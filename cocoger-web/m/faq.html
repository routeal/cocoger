What is Cocoger?

Cocoger is a location sharing app.

What's difference?

Cocoger allows you to share location by the polical entities i.e. town, city or state. When your setting is by city, you can locate your friend somewhere in city, vice versa to your friend. The device usually picks the geographical center for the selected political entity.

Who runs Cocoger?

Routeal, L.L.C.

The notification delays. Why?

While your app runs in the foreground, your movement is periodically updated so that your friend receives the notification almost in real time. However, while your app runs in the background, your movement is detected only when you move significantly. How significant? It varies depending on where you are. You get more update in city than out of town.

Facebook images won't be changed. Why?

All the images are cached. Logout, and login to reload the images.

My friend icons seem out of date. Why?

Due to some technical problem, the icons can be out of date. You can do either of:
  • Refresh the icons by the Fresh button in the toolbar.
  • Reload the friend list by dragging down in the Friend view.
